## System Overview
The system is a banking management system developed using object-oriented programming.
It manages customers, accounts, employees, loans, cards, and transactions.

## Actors
- Customer
- Employee
- Bank System

## Main Components
- BankAccount
- Customer
- Employee
- Loan
- BankCard
- TransactionProcessor
- BankSystem

## System Functions
- Create and manage bank accounts
- Deposit and withdraw money
- Manage loans and cards
- Store customer and employee data

## Design Principles
The system applies object-oriented principles such as encapsulation, abstraction, and the Open-Closed Principle.
